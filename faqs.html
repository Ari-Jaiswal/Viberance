Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting your custom wallpaper

What do you use to make these Wallpapers?

I personally use Canvas for organising and mainly making the wallpaper, while using Pinterest and Google to find images and content for your wallpaper and often other apps and sites for more specific things.

Can I give you personal images or text for the wallpaper?

Yeah sure! It makes your wallpaper so much more personal for you since it holds a part of you excluding your theme and content that I put.

Can you not post my wallpaper on Pinterest for my personal reasons?

I totally understand if you're uncomfortable with your wallpaper being out there on the internet and I have no issue with not posting it, just specify it when messaging me that you don't want me to post it.

After getting my wallpaper, can I just casually talk with you?

Yup! I personally feel like connecting with new people beyond business is one of the best ways to find stable yet strong friendships.

What if I don't have a WhatsApp number?

In my 'Contact Me' page, there is my username for Discord which you can also opt for.

Is this like a full-time thing or just for fun?

This is just a fun hobby that I took a little seriously, so I don't charge at all or accept money since this is just for fun.
